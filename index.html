Profit Calculation in Python

Simple function to determine net profit.


                    def calculate_net_profit(revenue, expenses):
                        """Calculates net profit from revenue and expenses."""
                        return revenue - expenses

                    # Example usage for Q3 ($15M revenue, $11.7M expenses)
                    q3_profit = calculate_net_profit(15000000, 11700000)

Growth Analysis: CAGR Formula

We use the Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) for a smoothed average.

\[ CAGR = \left( \frac{\text{Ending Value}}{\text{Beginning Value}} \right)^{\frac{1}{\text{Number of Years}}} - 1 \]

Speaker notes: Mention that our 5-year revenue CAGR is a healthy 12.8%, indicating consistent and stable growth, which is a key takeaway for our stakeholders. This outperforms the industry average.
